Puri

  

Puri is a coastal city and a municipality in the state of Odisha in eastern India.
It is the district headquarters of Puri district and is situated on the Bay of Bengal, 60 kilometres (37 mi) south of the state capital of Bhubaneswar.
It is home to the 12th-century Jagannath Temple
and is one of the original Char Dham pilgrimage sites for Hindus.
